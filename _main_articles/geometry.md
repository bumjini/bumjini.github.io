The arithmetic mean 

$$
AM = \frac{x_1+\cdots x_n}{n}
$$

The geometric mean 

$$
GM = \sqrt[^n]{x_1+\cdots x_n} 
$$

We have 

$$
AM \ge GM \\ 
\frac{x_1+\cdots x_n}{n} \ge  \sqrt[^n]{x_1+\cdots x_n} 
$$
